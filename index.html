How we choose our picks

We don’t test mattresses in a lab or run timed trials. Instead, our picks are based on a careful synthesis of long‑term owner feedback from verified buyers, published reviews from trusted expert sources, and our own analysis of brand reputation, construction quality, and customer service records. We look for patterns in what owners say breaks, or doesn’t, after two, three, or five years. Durability, edge support, motion isolation, and temperature regulation are the factors we weigh most heavily. We also consider how easy the company makes returns, exchanges, and warranty claims, because a good mattress is only as good as the company standing behind it. Every recommendation also accounts for a range of sleep positions and body types to ensure broad usefulness.
