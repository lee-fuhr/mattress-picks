How we choose our picks

We don’t test beds ourselves. Instead, we scour thousands of long-term owner reviews, talk to independent repair technicians, and consult published expert analyses to find what actually holds up over years of use. We look for patterns: which beds develop sagging in year three? Which ones sleep hot despite marketing claims? Price is never our sole criterion. A cheap mattress that fails quickly costs more than a moderately priced one that lasts. We balance comfort ratings, return policies, warranty terms, and materials transparency.
